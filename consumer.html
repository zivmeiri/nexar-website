Frequently asked
Questions and answers
Do I need a subscription to use my Nexar dashcam?

No. Every Nexar dashcam works out of the box with free cloud backup via the Nexar app. The optional LTE plan ($9.99/mo) adds cellular connectivity, live view, and real-time alerts, but it is not required.

What happens if my camera is damaged in an accident?

Your footage is already in the cloud. Nexar syncs clips automatically, so even if the camera is destroyed, your evidence is safe and accessible from the app.

How do I install the dashcam?

Mount the magnetic base to your windshield, attach the camera, and plug into your car's USB or 12V outlet. Download the Nexar app, pair via Bluetooth, and you are recording. The entire process takes about 3 minutes.

Does Nexar record when my car is parked?

Yes. With parking mode enabled, the camera activates when it detects motion or an impact. You receive a notification on your phone with the captured footage.

How much storage do I get?

Nexar provides free cloud storage for all incident clips and important moments. Continuous recording loops on the SD card (included). With the LTE plan, storage is expanded and clips are uploaded in real time.

Can I share footage with my insurance company?

Yes. Tap any clip in the Nexar app and share it via email, text, or direct download. Many insurance companies accept Nexar footage as evidence for claims.

What is the video quality?

Beam2 records in 1080p Full HD. Nexar One records in 4K for the front camera and 1080p for interior and rear cameras. All models include HDR for clear footage in any lighting condition.

Does Nexar work in extreme temperatures?

Yes. Nexar cameras are designed to withstand temperatures from -4°F to 158°F (-20°C to 70°C). A built-in supercapacitor replaces lithium batteries for safer operation in hot climates.

Will the dashcam drain my car battery?

No. Nexar cameras draw minimal power and include a built-in voltage cutoff that prevents your car battery from draining below a safe level.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your footage is encrypted end-to-end and belongs to you. Nexar never sells personal video. All data practices are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.

What is the return policy?

Nexar offers a 30-day return policy for unused cameras in original packaging. All cameras include a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty options are available at checkout.

Can I use Nexar without the app?

The camera records independently as soon as it has power. However, the free Nexar app is required for cloud backup, sharing clips, and accessing features like real-time alerts and parking notifications.
