Frequently asked
Fleet questions, answered.
How long does it take to deploy across my fleet?

Most fleets are fully operational within 1 to 2 weeks. Each camera installs in under 10 minutes. No professional installation, no wiring, no downtime. Our team handles onboarding and training remotely.

Is there a minimum fleet size?

No. Nexar Fleet works for fleets of 5 vehicles or 5,000. Pricing scales linearly with no volume minimums or tiered gates. You add vehicles as you grow.

What kind of support do I get?

A dedicated account manager who knows your fleet. Phone support, email support, and proactive check-ins. We handle hardware replacements, software updates, and driver onboarding materials.

Does the camera work without cellular coverage?

Yes. The camera records locally and syncs to the cloud when connectivity returns. No footage is ever lost, even in areas with spotty cellular coverage.

Can I use Nexar with my existing fleet management tools?

Nexar integrates with major fleet management platforms via API. Your Nexar data can flow into your existing workflows. Contact our team for integration details specific to your setup.
